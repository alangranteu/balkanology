Republic of Macedonia :: Books

Travel Guides

Short descriptions of Macedonia appear in three of Lonely Planet's regional guides listed in the Overview section: Europe on a Shoestring, Eastern Europe, and Southeastern Europe.

Macedonia - The Bradt Guide  (5th edition published April 2015) - Thammy Evans

Many travellers spend only a few days in Macedonia, visiting Ohrid while in transit between other countries; for this purpose any of the above guides should be sufficient. If you would like to explore the country in more depth (which is highly recommended), you will probably be better off with the Bradt Guide.
