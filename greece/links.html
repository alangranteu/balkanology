Mainland Greece :: Links

In contrast to most other countries covered by Balkanology, I have not investigated even a handful of the thousands of sites devoted to travel in Greece. If you need to know more, the first site listed (Greece Travel Guide) should point you in the right direction. Many of the more general sites listed in the Overview of the Balkans are also useful for planning a trip to Greece.

Travel Information

  • Greece Travel Guide

    This personal site has hundreds of pages of advice about Greece (islands and mainland), together with articles on such topics as hiking in Delphi, Winter in Greece, and Greek Cinema. Beware: if you are considering a trip to Greece you may end up spending hours on this site.

Transport and Practicalities

  • OSE - Hellenic Railways Organisation

    Timetable and fare information for Greece's domestic trains. The site appears to undergo frequent redesigns, becoming a little more difficult to use each time.

  • Greek Ferries

    Database covering both domestic and international ferry routes, with online booking.

  • KTEL

    This site formerly had timetables for buses throughout Greece. Unfortunately this service has been withdrawn, and replaced with a premium-rate phone service available in Greek only - a strange situation for a country with significant earnings from foreign tourism. This makes advance planning of bus journeys in Greece rather difficult. The gap is partially filled by websites run by provincial KTEL organisations, local authorities, and local tourist organisations, each of which provides information about a small subset of bus routes. There is a very useful list of these sites on the Living in Greece site.
